About Us

Pesa Mashinani is an innovative platform dedicated to connecting certified veterinarians with those seeking reliable veterinary care, including farmers, pet owners, and agrovet shop owners.

Our mission is to ensure accessible, high-quality veterinary services throughout Kenya, enhancing animal health, supporting sustainable agriculture, and empowering communities.

Through our mobile app, web platform, and USSD service for non-smartphone users, Vet Konnect ensures that anyone, anywhere, can connect with certified veterinarians efficiently.

Our goal is to significantly reduce animal disease and mortality rates by providing trustworthy veterinary care, preventing exploitation by unqualified practitioners, and equipping farmers and pet owners with essential tools to maintain animal health and productivity.

Find and book qualified veterinarians effortlessly through our comprehensive vet directory.

Access a marketplace for purchasing essential veterinary and animal care products.

Engage in a community forum for knowledge sharing and expert advice on animal care.

Support vital animal welfare programs, such as cattle dipping days, to enhance farm productivity and animal health.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Discover answers to common queries regarding our platform, services, and policies.

Booking an appointment is straightforward! Simply log in to your Vetkonnect account, browse our directory of qualified veterinarians, select one that meets your needs, and choose an available time slot. Confirm your booking, and you will receive a confirmation email with all the relevant details.

Yes, you can easily reschedule or cancel your appointment through your account. Just log in, navigate to "My Appointments," select the booking you wish to modify, and choose a new time or cancel altogether. Note: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the appointment to avoid any charges.

In emergencies, you can reach our 24/7 emergency hotline at 0712345678.

Once your pet is receiving care, you can monitor their treatment progress via our online portal. Veterinarians will regularly update records, and you'll receive notifications via email or text with important updates and recommendations.
